# NextGen Operations Brief

## A) What This Business Is

- Creator/talent management operation focused on OnlyFans/LoyalFans-style ecosystems.
- Growth strategy prioritizes off-platform acquisition channels.
- Operating model combines recruiting, deal closing, and management/coaching intervention.
- Business is framed as performance marketing + operations, not passive talent listing.
- Current repo context is strategic; numeric operating evidence files are missing.

## B) How Money Is Made

- **Managed creator revenue share**: `Collected Revenue = agency share of creator earnings`.
- **Closed deals/partnerships**: `Collected Revenue = net agency revenue from executed deals`.
- **Optimization lift**: `Incremental Revenue = baseline revenue vs post-intervention revenue lift`.

## C) Operating Flow (Lead → Close → Deliver → Expand)

1. Source creators and partners from target channels.
2. Qualify fit, readiness, and commercial potential.
3. Close agreement and onboarding terms.
4. Deliver management, coaching, and execution support.
5. Expand through retention, upsell services, and performance lift.

## E) Roles

### Executive Account Manager (sales + lead gen)

- Closes partner/deal opportunities and owns revenue-side account development.

### Recruiter

- Sources creators, qualifies candidates, and drives signed onboarding.

### Management/Coaching

- Runs optimization interventions and monitors measurable revenue lift.

## F) Lead Gen + Closing Playbook

1. Run recruiter outreach by channel and persona.
2. Conduct qualification call/chat and readiness scoring.
3. Align offer model and operating expectations.
4. Close agreement and begin onboarding workflow.
5. Execute first 30-day management plan.
6. Review performance and expansion options.

## G) How Results Are Measured by Role

### Recruiter

- **Qualified creators/week**: creator candidates who pass persona fit, content consistency, and onboarding-readiness checks.
- **Recruiter-to-sign conversion**: percentage of recruiter-qualified creators who sign agreements.
- **Activation speed**: median days from signature to first managed content cycle.

### Executive Account Manager

- **Net new managed revenue/week**: collected agency-share revenue from newly closed creators/partnerships.
- **Close efficiency**: percentage of qualified opportunities closed inside the target cycle time.
- **Retention revenue stability**: month-over-month collected revenue for accounts closed by the EAM.

### Management / Coaching

- **Intervention lift**: percentage increase in collected creator revenue 30 and 90 days after intervention.
- **Net creator retention**: share of managed creators who maintain or grow monthly revenue.
- **Operational SLA adherence**: percentage of critical creator blockers resolved within the agreed time window.
