## F) Lead Gen + Closing Playbook

1. Intake inquiry with timeline + desired deliverables.
2. Qualify budget and best-fit package.
3. Present package plus add-on options.
4. Close booking and collect payment/deposit.
5. Deliver service and capture proof assets.
6. Offer follow-up add-ons and referral incentive.

## G) How Results Are Measured by Role

### Executive Account Manager

- **Qualified inquiry-to-booking rate**: percentage of qualified inquiries converted to paid package bookings.
- **Average booked package value**: collected revenue per booking before add-ons.
- **Add-on attachment rate**: share of bookings that include at least one upsell service.
- **60-day repeat/referral conversion**: percentage of delivered clients who rebook or refer a paying client.
- **Collection completion rate**: percentage of booked revenue fully collected inside payment terms.

### Management / Delivery Oversight (if active)

- **On-time delivery adherence**: percentage of projects completed by promised handoff date.
- **Revision containment**: percentage of projects delivered without scope-breaking revisions.
- **Post-delivery satisfaction signal**: percentage of delivered clients providing positive testimonial/referral-ready feedback.
