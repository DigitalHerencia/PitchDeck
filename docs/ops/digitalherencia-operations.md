## F) Lead Gen + Closing Playbook

1. Build target account list by vertical/problem fit.
2. Outbound and referral activation.
3. Discovery focused on revenue bottlenecks.
4. Offer scoped plan with delivery milestones.
5. Close on commercial terms.
6. Run onboarding and first-value milestone.
7. Present retainer/expansion path.

## G) How Results Are Measured by Role

### Executive Account Manager

- **Qualified pipeline value/week**: dollar value of ICP-fit accounts that complete discovery and pass buying criteria.
- **Proposal-to-close rate**: percentage of proposals converted into signed contracts.
- **Time-to-first-value**: days from signature to first client-accepted delivery milestone.
- **90-day gross revenue retention**: retained recurring revenue from accounts closed by the EAM.
- **Expansion conversion rate**: percentage of retained accounts purchasing additional scope within the quarter.

### Management / Delivery Oversight (when assigned)

- **Delivery predictability**: percentage of milestones delivered on or before committed date.
- **Implementation quality**: percentage of launches with no critical rework in first 30 days.
- **Expansion readiness**: percentage of accounts with documented ROI review and expansion offer prepared by renewal window.
